A discussion by Vanessa Lyon on the path to value and how to deal with the risks of an emerging technology. Vanessa is a Managing Director and Senior Partner at Boston Consulting Group in New York. She is an expert on large platform derisking at BCG. She leads the firm's work in cyber and digital risk globally, including the BCG Center for Leadership in Cyber Strategy, and is a core member of the firm's Risk & Compliance and Tech & Digital Advantage practices.
