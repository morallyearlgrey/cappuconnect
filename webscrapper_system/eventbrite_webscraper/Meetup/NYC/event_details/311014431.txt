Join Woodbridge Toastmasters Club on Zoom! Guests and first-timers are always welcomed. Fellow Toastmasters are also welcomed.

Every 1st, 3rd, and 5th Thursday

Woodbridge Toastmasters Club meetings are filled with the excitement of discovering hidden talents, making lasting relationships, and achieving personal goals. This is a self-paced informal program that is designed to allow individuals to tailor their progress through a series of projects which gradually increase in complexity and challenges, in a chosen Pathway.

If you want to learn how to be a better speaker and leader, you ought to be a member of Woodbridge Toastmasters Club!
