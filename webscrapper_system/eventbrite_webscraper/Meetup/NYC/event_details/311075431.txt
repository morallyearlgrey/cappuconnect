Join us for an interview with sound designer Sabrina Fedele! Sabrina has worked on games like Airborne Empire, Lichgate, and Letterhead and has spoken at venues such as GameSoundCon, AirCon, and USC. We'll be diving into how surreal narratives like those of Twin Peaks and Silent Hill 2 have influenced her approach, and how her love of wildlife has informed her field recording and custom soundscapes.
