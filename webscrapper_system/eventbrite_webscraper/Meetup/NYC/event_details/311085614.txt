Join us for this engaging and supportive Meetup event designed to help you move forward from past relationship experiences with confidence and clarity.

Share and listen to stories in a safe, non-judgmental environment.
Learn strategies to manage emotional stress and let go of lingering negativity from past relationships.
Meet like-minded individuals who are also on a journey to create healthier, happier relationships.

This event is perfect for anyone who wants to reduce stress, gain fresh insights, and prepare for meaningful connections. Whether you're single, recently out of a relationship, or just seeking personal growth, this is a welcoming space to learn and grow.
Let go of the past and take the first step toward a brighter future.
This group is created by the Dianetics & Scientology life improvement center.
