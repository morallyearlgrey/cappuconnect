Kushi Kushi Yaki is foremost a Japanese grilled skewer restaurant and they also serve classic ramen options and bento box options. Please take a look at the menu here (https://www.kushikushiyaki.com/menu). The restaurant is inspired by "back alley restaurants in Japanese cities." Kushi Kushi Yaki is located right near the heart of KTown in NYC!

Please bring cash to pay for your meal, thank you.
