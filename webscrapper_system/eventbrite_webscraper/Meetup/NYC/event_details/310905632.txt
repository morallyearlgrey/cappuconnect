If you're an indie game developer, switching from the mindset of creating your game to showing your game at an event can be a new and intimidating experience. This collaborative talk will feature volunteers from the IGDA DC chapter giving lightning talks about their tips and tricks for presenting their game at events. Learn from people who have done it before so you can be prepared to showcase your creativity to the world!
