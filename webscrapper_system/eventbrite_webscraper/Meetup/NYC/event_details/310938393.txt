A family-owned, third-generation restaurant in Greenwich Village, Village Taverna blends classic and contemporary Greek fare. Along with skordalia, taramosalata and tzatziki, specialties include grilled octopus, calamari, and lamb shank. The full menu appears here: https://www.villagetaverna.com/menus.html. Don't miss the souvlaki in particular: https://www.theinfatuation.com/new-york/reviews/village-taverna
