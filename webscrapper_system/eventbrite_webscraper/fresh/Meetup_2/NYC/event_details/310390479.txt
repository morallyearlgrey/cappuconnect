Join the Friendly Cigar Smokers of NYC for a relaxing evening at one of our favorite cigar bar ( SoHo) . Whether you are a seasoned cigar aficionado or new to the world of cigars, this event is perfect for anyone looking to enjoy great company and quality smokes. Indulge in a variety of fine cigars paired with your choice of premium scotch, bourbon, or cocktails. Share stories, make new friends, and unwind in a cozy and cigar-friendly setting.

Let's make this summer even more memorable as we gather on the 25th of September to celebrate friendship, good times, and the joy of cigar smoking.
