Folk Dance Thursday nights in Brooklyn Heights
Brooklyn Heights International Folk Dancers every Thursday evening from 7pm - 9:15pm.
Come dance your favorites at Zion Lutheran Church, 125 Henry St. in Brooklyn Heights (Basement entrance).

Admission: $10 for weekly sessions, every Thursday (except Thanksgiving, 12/26, 1/2).

SUBWAYS:
* 2/3 Clark Street;
* A/C to High Street;
* R to Court St;
* 4/5 to Borough Hall

INVITE YOUR FRIENDS!
