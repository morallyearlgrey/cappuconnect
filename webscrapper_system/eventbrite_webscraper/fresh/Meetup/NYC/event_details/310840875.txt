Bring pages from your novel, screenplay, short story, poetry or non-fiction - read out loud and get immediate feedback on your project. Get together with your fellow authors, screenwriters and filmmakers and talk through any hurdles or roadblocks.

This meetup is free and open to everyone. Outro NYC asks that everyone buy at least one drink.

If possible, bring around 10 copies of your project for everyone to read. Ideally 2000 words / 10 script pages or less. It may be possible to read through longer projects depending on the number of submissions. Also ok to just listen in if you don't have anything to submit.
