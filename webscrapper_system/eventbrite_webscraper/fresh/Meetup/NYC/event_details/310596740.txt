The concept of our language exchange event is to provide a relaxed and friendly atmosphere for a gathering of folks to practice Spanish/English whilst making new friends and sharing experiences with people from different parts of the world.

Please look for an organizer when you arrive and we'll help you make a nametag and get you seated.

The event fee is $3 (cash or Venmo suggested**)** when you arrive.

Please note, that this is not a dating event so please avoid any kind of interaction that may make other attendees feel uncomfortable. If you feel like someone is acting inappropriately, please approach one of the organizers so that we can handle it.

Sometimes we take photos at our events to preserve memory of our gatherings and for social media promotion.
