Join us for an immersive day delving into the fascinating world where culture, digital art, and augmented reality intersect. Our meetup, hosted by the New York AR/AI/WEB3/Digital Artists group, will feature a day full of digital art and hands-on experiences that showcase the innovative ways in which artists are leveraging AR technology to explore and interpret different cultures through their art.

Whether you are an established digital artist, a tech enthusiast, or simply curious about the evolving landscape of art and technology, this event promises to inspire and spark creativity.

Connect with like-minded individuals, share insights, and gain practical knowledge about the limitless possibilities of incorporating AR into digital art, showcased through a digital AR art walk through Bryant Park. Don't miss this opportunity to be a part of this forward-thinking community and transform your perspective on the future of art in the digital age.
