Today's artist in the music and recording industry have more creative control over their music, their songs and their creative direction.
With this. control comes the responsibility of developing the skills needed to execute a music project. The artist now has to take on the role of singer, songwriter, producer, engineer, and marketing specialist. These roles were. previously delegated by production managers and record labels.

The "Songwriting & Recording Techniques from Start to Finish" class is about learning the different skills needed to complete a professional and commercial music project.

This class offers artists a community of other artists, industry professionals, resources and information to assist them with the creative process.
